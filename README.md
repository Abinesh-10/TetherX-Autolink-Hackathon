🌟 Overview

Cruise Autolink is a digital fleet management platform designed to simplify vehicle registration, authorization, and rental management. The system allows fleet owners to securely manage vehicle records and seamlessly connect authorized vehicles to a rental portal where customers can easily book available vehicles.

The goal of the platform is to transform traditional fleet management into a smart, automated, and scalable ecosystem.

🎯 Problem Statement

Many fleet operators still manage vehicle records such as registration, insurance, and policies manually. This often results in:

⚠️ Poor record tracking
⚠️ Inefficient vehicle utilization
⚠️ Delays in vehicle authorization
⚠️ Difficulty connecting fleets with rental services

Cruise Autolink solves this by creating a centralized digital fleet ecosystem.

🏗️ System Architecture

The platform follows a multi-layer architecture:

1️⃣ Vehicle Owner registers vehicle details
2️⃣ System verifies registration, insurance, and policies
3️⃣ Authorized vehicles are stored in the fleet database
4️⃣ Vehicles are listed on the rental portal
5️⃣ Customers browse and book available vehicles

This architecture ensures secure data management and efficient fleet operations.
